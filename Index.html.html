Baby Blue

Entering an open window into room filled with books from bio to pre-cal a chirp can be heard. Standing at bedside onto a wooden task a blue bird stares at the cracked open window trap in shady black cage.

Appropriately named blue for it's blue feathers. Blue looks through its cage at the open window viewing the sykscrapers.

The Iron Box

Swooping around the box chomping at it's bars, Blue struggles to open the small door that only the weird human who comes once a while can open it. Pausing, Blue stands in the middle chirping while still eyeing at the shut window with huge concrete structures in the far distance.

The Human

Door slams open as the human wearing glass around her eyes rushes and picks up several books and shoves them into her bag as she bites down a piece of bread thats in a metal warming box. She leaves but dashes back inside towards Blue's cage and rambles something apologetically,picking the feeder and refilling it afterwords running off.
